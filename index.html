My Story

Always into tech

  • 1985

    Not so long ago (according to me)

    First time I touched a computer was in 4th grade. An apple IIe in my math class. Growing up in Puerto Rico made this a rarity. Aquiring one was way out of my parents reach, but I was instantly hooked!

  • Late 1980's

    Fun growing up

    No computer at home meant staying busy oustide skate boarding for the rest of the 1980's. Lots of bandaids on the knees and elbows, but great memories!

  • 1992

    My first computer

    My Autocad class in high school had 286's and now I had a 386 at home. I practically had to wear a seat belt when using this thing. 2MB of RAM and I can format high density floppies what!

  • 2000's

    Y2K and beyond

    Started my IT career supporting desktops and servers. Later, moved onto networks and Voice over IP. Now coding has taken over me and it couldn't be more awesome. Definitely saved the best for last.
